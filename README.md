<h2>How to Play the Math Game</h2>

<p><strong>Choose a Math Mode</strong><br>
Select one of the tabs at the top: Addition, Subtraction, Multiplication, or Division.</p>

<p><strong>Answer the Question</strong><br>
A math question (e.g. 8 + 45 = ?) will appear in the center of the screen.<br>
Type your answer into the Your Answer input box.</p>

<p><strong>Submit Your Answer</strong><br>
Click Submit Answer to check your response.</p>

<ul>
  <li>Correct answers are counted as <strong>Correct</strong></li>
  <li>Wrong answers are counted as <strong>Incorrect</strong></li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Move to the Next Question</strong><br>
Click Next Question to continue playing and challenge yourself with a new problem.</p>

<p><strong>Review Past Questions</strong><br>
Scroll down to see your Past Questions, where:</p>

<ul>
  <li>✅ Green highlights indicate correct answers</li>
  <li>❌ Red highlights indicate incorrect answers</li>
  <li>Each entry shows the operation, question, and correct result</li>
</ul>
